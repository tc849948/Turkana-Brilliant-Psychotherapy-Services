About TBPS

Community-Based Psychotherapy Services

Turkana Brilliant Psychotherapy Services (TBPS) is a nonprofit organization founded in 2019 striving to provide psychosocial services to the general population of Turkana County.

TBPS services are handled by mental health professionals, psychotherapists, psychologists, and clinical officers; but often they expand the services through the use of student psychologists, student social workers and student counselors who are supervised by experienced professionals.

What is a Counseling Psychologist?

A counseling psychologist specializes in providing therapy, guidance, and support to individuals, couples, families, and groups. They focus on helping people cope with emotional, social, vocational, and educational challenges, aiming to enhance their overall well-being and quality of life. Counseling psychologists often work in various settings such as private practice, mental health clinics, schools, colleges, or community organizations.

Counseling psychologists utilize a range of therapeutic techniques and approaches to address their clients' concerns. They create a safe and supportive environment where clients can explore their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors. Counseling psychologists help individuals develop insights, coping strategies, and problem-solving skills to navigate through life's difficulties, manage stress, and improve their mental and emotional health. They may address issues such as anxiety, depression, relationship problems, career transitions, grief, and self-esteem.

Transparency

Scope of Work

Individual Counseling Sessions:

We Conduct one-on-one counseling sessions with clients to provide personalized emotional and psychological support. These sessions are tailored to address individual concerns, such as trauma, anxiety, and self-esteem issues.

Support Groups:

We Organize group counseling sessions where clients can connect with peers facing similar challenges. These support groups offer a platform for sharing experiences, offering mutual encouragement, and developing a sense of community.

Workshops on Coping Strategies and Self-Care:

We Host workshops focused on practical coping strategies, stress management, and self-care techniques. These workshops provide teenage mothers with tools to manage their stress, improve their mental health, and prioritize their well-being.

Our Comprehensive Services Include:

  • Creating awareness on communicable and non-communicable diseases e.g. TB, HIV/AIDS
  • Training/capacity building on prevention measures, causal effects of communicable non communicable diseases
  • Counselling clients with; depression, anxiety, family issues and stress related disorders
  • Managing clients behavioral and developmental disorders
  • Link complicated mental health disorders e.g. Dementia, schizophrenia and other neurocognitive disorders to experts
  • Promote healthy behavior in the community through behavior change programs
  • Create awareness on danger of alcohol addiction and substance abuse to a person's life
  • Train on sexual gender-based violence, causes and effects to individual and community at large
  • Maternal health care and family planning guidelines
  • Current and emerging health related issues e.g. Covid 19 pandemic
  • Health and sanitation principles
  • Medication management and drug addiction
